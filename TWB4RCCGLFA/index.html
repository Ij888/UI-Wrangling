Meet the Team

The Living Faith Assembly choir is a very important part of the church. Every Sunday, they minister in praise,worship and thanksgiving. Every first Sunday of the month,we have swahili Sunday where we worship only in our ethnic languages. A highlight of the choir's year is the Charity Praise Night, where we all get together and praise God through our different cultures.
The Youth of Living Faith Assembly are a close knit family who strive towards building up and exercising their faith in the Lord Jesus Christ, as the name of the church implies. We try to make an impact in our community through different activities, praying in faith that, starting from our own community, we will win our generation and indeed the whole nation for Christ.
